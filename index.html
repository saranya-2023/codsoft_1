CAKES

Cakes are delightful desserts often made from flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, with a variety of flavors and decorations. They come in numerous types, including sponge cakes, cheesecakes, and fruitcakes, catering to diverse tastes. Cakes are a staple in celebrations like birthdays, weddings, and holidays, symbolizing joy and festivity. Decorating cakes with icing, fondant, and edible flowers has become an art form, showcasing creativity and skill. With endless possibilities for flavor combinations and designs, cakes remain a beloved treat enjoyed by people of all ages.
